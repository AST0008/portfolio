Recently I have come to realise the fact that we cant have everything in life. If you see something which has any other person has and for some reason you think you cannot it get it, it is more often than not your fault. Life is about trade offs. If any other person is having somehthing, wheather it be any physical item or relationships, he has sacrificed something he has in order to get that.

Also how weird is our brain, we always crave things which we dont have, grass is greener on the other side. All of the major negative emotions and thoughts arise from the fact we are not happy/contempt with what we have.

Life is all about trade-offs, about proritizing what we choose to have at any given time over the other thing. Everyone one of the great person which we admire has in some form sacrificed things which we're completely oblivious too. 

In my class there is a student, lets call him A. He is the smartest person in the class, always tops, is very intellectually above everyone else. I always want to be like him, but am I willing to sacrifice all of the things which I have currently to be like him? , probably not, I have a good life, good friends, good hobbies?, etc, but at the same time I cant help but think on how life would like on the other side of the road. And this thought has been bugging me my entire teenage/adult life.

I'm a great overthinker, mostly likely the best in the business, something which I am not proud of. I overthink the smallest desicion like a million times. I dont know why im like that, mostly cause I was always raised like that. People think im silent and patient and would like to do things alone, which is not true at all. I just cant help but overthink everything such that it is possibly quite impossible for me to be normal. 

Even when life is good, even when everything seems to be going perfect, I stay there being miserable, feeling guilty that I dont deserve this life which I have got. That im wasting my potential every second in which im not doing anything, not being productive every moment of my life.
