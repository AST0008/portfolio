I recently completed watching Succession S1 (2nd rerun) and I completely forgot how good it. Every single character has great storyline and importance. That made me realise you know which tv show is actually the greatest?

I have a few nominees, one of them being __Breaking Bad__, I think its definetly the most popular out of all the great shows, also it has the most perfect ending to any tv show I have ever watched. The character arc of walter is some insane writing.

The other one of the argument I think is definately __Game of Thrones__. Its ending was very rushed but overall the entire tv show was perfect. From the amazing scenes of dragons, to the wisdom of Tyrion Lannister, everything was good in each episode. At the end of each episode you're left in awe of the production quality.

The last one I think is The Soparanos, without which breaking bad woudn't exist. The acting of Tony, the developement of side characters, to the amazing dialogues, everything is brilliant. This show feels the most real out of all, and you are most likely to binge it completely once you start it.

So which is it?, For me I think its __Sopranos__, cause how everything from start to finish is good, it doesn't end poorly like GoT, nor is it slightly slow in the start like  breaking bad. Honestly I also think it depends on when and where you watch the show.  


## [Sitcoms](#sitcoms)

As far as 20 mins show goes, there are a lot of options, cause there are so many generes and themes in which you can pick. 

Personally I dont like __Friends__. I dont like the forced audience laughter and for similar reason I dont like __TBBT__. 

My personal favourite is __How I Met Your Mother__. I think every character is funny and relatable in their own way. Also something about the concept of narrating your life story as show feels unique and keeps you always wondering when will he met his wife. 

But for the Greatest if I had to pick one, it would be __The Office__. It has the 2 of the most funniest TV characters in Micheal and Dwight, that along with the side love of Jim and Pam, with other side characters being equally humurous make it a perfect which  you binge watch. It wasn't that peak after michael left but still quite good.
